A friend of mine used to drive over the Blue Ridge Mountains every day to work for over 25 years. One time I was riding with him and the views were breathtaking! I asked him one time if after all these years, if he ever grew tired of the view. He replied "Never! I see something new every day. In all these years, it's never been the same."

Morning Shadows started because I wanted to wake up every day and see something new. Every day, the sun comes into out windows and casts these intricate shadows on our wall. I try to capture the beauty I see in them.
